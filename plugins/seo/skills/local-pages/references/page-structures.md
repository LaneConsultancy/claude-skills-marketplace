# Page Structures for Local Business Pages

Detailed templates with word count targets for service pages and location pages.

## Service Page Template

**Target word count:** 1000-1500 words (minimum 1000)

### Full Structure

```
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TITLE TAG (50-60 characters)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
[Primary Keyword] + [Benefit/Location] | [Business Name]

Example: Same-Day Boiler Repair in Dartford | Lane Heating

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
META DESCRIPTION (150-155 characters)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
[Problem/Solution]. [Proof/Differentiator]. [CTA].

Example: Boiler broken? Fixed same-day with upfront pricing. Gas Safe
registered engineer serving Dartford & North Kent. Call now for fast response.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
H1 HEADING
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
[Primary Keyword] + [Key Differentiator or Benefit]

Examples:
- Same-Day Boiler Repair in Dartford
- Emergency Boiler Repairs — Fixed Prices, No Call-Out Fee
- Professional Boiler Repair by Gas Safe Engineers

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
OPENING LEAD (100-150 words)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Address the reader's situation immediately. State your core promise.
Include primary keyword in first 100 words naturally.

Structure:
- Sentence 1: Acknowledge their problem/situation
- Sentence 2-3: Your promise/solution
- Sentence 4-5: Why you (credentials, local presence)
- Sentence 6: Soft CTA or next step

Example:
"When your boiler breaks down, you need it fixed fast — not three
days from now. I'm a Gas Safe registered engineer based in Greenhithe,
and I can usually be with you within 2 hours for emergency repairs.

I'll diagnose the problem, give you a fixed price before starting work,
and in most cases, have your heating back on the same day. No call-out
charges if I can't fix it."

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
H2: What We Do / How [Service] Works (200-300 words)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Include keyword variation in heading.

Cover:
- What the service involves (plain English)
- The process from their perspective
- What they can expect (timeframes, what happens)
- Types/variations of the service you offer

Example heading: "How Our Boiler Repair Service Works"

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
H2: Why Choose [Business Name] (200-300 words)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Include secondary keyword if natural.

Cover:
- Key differentiators (be specific, not generic)
- Credentials with reason-why
- Guarantees or promises with specifics
- Social proof (review stats, testimonials)

Use bullets for scanability:
- ✓ Gas Safe registered (#123456) — legally required for gas work
- ✓ Fixed prices quoted before work starts
- ✓ Parts warranty: 12 months on all replacement parts
- ✓ 4.9 stars from 127 Google reviews

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
H2: Common [Service] Problems We Fix (200-300 words)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Include keyword variation. Demonstrates expertise.

List specific problems/situations:
- No hot water / no heating
- Boiler keeps cutting out
- Strange noises (banging, whistling)
- Error codes (reference common ones)
- Pressure loss issues
- Pilot light problems

Brief explanation of each shows expertise. Can use H3s for each problem.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
H2: Frequently Asked Questions About [Primary Keyword] (150-250 words)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Include primary keyword. FAQ schema opportunity.

3-5 questions addressing:
- Pricing concerns
- Timeframes
- Common objections
- Technical questions readers have

Format as Q&A pairs. Mark up with FAQ schema.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
H2: Areas We Cover (50-100 words)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Include location keyword.

Example: "[Service] in [Location] and Surrounding Areas"

List nearby towns, postcodes, neighbourhoods. Can include brief travel
time context ("within 30 minutes of our Greenhithe base").

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
H2: Get Your [Service] Sorted Today (50-100 words)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Clear CTA section.

Include:
- Phone number (prominent)
- Alternative contact method (form, email)
- What happens when they call
- Reassurance (no obligation quote, etc.)
```

## Location Page Template

**Target word count:** 1000-1500 words (minimum 1000)

Location pages must be substantive — not thin content with swapped town names.

### Full Structure

```
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TITLE TAG (50-60 characters)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
[Primary Service] in [Location] | [Business Name]

Example: Plumber in Dartford | Lane Plumbing Services

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
META DESCRIPTION (150-155 characters)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
[Location]-specific value proposition with CTA.

Example: Local plumber serving Dartford, Crayford & Bexley. Same-day
emergency call-outs, boiler repairs, and bathroom installations. Call now.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
H1 HEADING
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
[Primary Service] in [Location]

Examples:
- Plumber in Dartford
- Your Local Heating Engineer in Dartford
- Dartford Plumbing Services

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
OPENING LEAD (100-150 words)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Location-specific opening. Establish local presence and credibility.

Include:
- Acknowledge location-specific context if relevant
- Your local presence (where you're based, how long serving area)
- Core services overview
- Why local matters for this service

Example:
"Looking for a reliable plumber in Dartford? I've been serving Dartford
and the surrounding areas for over 15 years, from emergency call-outs
to full bathroom installations.

Based just 10 minutes away in Greenhithe, I can usually be with you
within the hour for emergencies. Whether it's a leaking pipe, broken
boiler, or a new bathroom project, I'll give you a fixed price upfront
and get the job done right."

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
H2: [Service 1] in [Location] (250-350 words)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Full service description for this location.

Structure each service section:
1. What the service is and why it matters (50-75 words)
2. Common situations/problems this addresses (75-100 words)
3. Your approach and what customers can expect (75-100 words)
4. Credentials and proof for this service (50-75 words)

Example: "Boiler Repair in Dartford"

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
H2: [Service 2] in [Location] (250-350 words)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Follow same structure. Different service.

Example: "Boiler Installation in Dartford"

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
H2: [Service 3] in [Location] (250-350 words)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Follow same structure. Different service.

Example: "Boiler Servicing in Dartford"

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
H2: [Service 4] in [Location] (250-350 words) — OPTIONAL
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Add if needed to reach word count or if business offers 4+ core services.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
H2: Why [Location] Residents Choose [Business Name] (150-200 words)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Local social proof and differentiators.

Include:
- Local testimonial if available
- Review stats
- Local credentials or associations
- How local presence benefits them

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
H2: Areas We Cover Near [Location] (50-100 words)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Nearby towns, neighbourhoods, postcodes.

Helps with "near me" and related location searches. Include:
- Neighbouring towns
- Postcode areas
- Well-known neighbourhoods or areas

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CTA SECTION (50-75 words)
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Contact details and next step.

Phone number, alternative contact, what happens when they call.
```

## Heading Keyword Integration

### Service Page Headings

```
H1: [Primary Keyword] + [Differentiator]
    └─ H2: How Our [Keyword Variation] Works
    └─ H2: Why Choose Us for [Keyword Variation]
    └─ H2: Common [Related Keyword] Problems We Fix
         └─ H3: [Specific Problem 1]
         └─ H3: [Specific Problem 2]
    └─ H2: [Primary Keyword] FAQ
    └─ H2: [Service] in [Location] and Nearby Areas
    └─ H2: Book Your [Service] Today
```

### Location Page Headings

```
H1: [Primary Service] in [Location]
    └─ H2: [Service 1] in [Location]
    └─ H2: [Service 2] in [Location]
    └─ H2: [Service 3] in [Location]
    └─ H2: Why [Location] Residents Choose [Business Name]
    └─ H2: Areas We Cover Near [Location]
```

Every H2 should contain either the primary keyword, a variation, or the location.

These catch most day-to-day tasks:

- **[Personal Admin Tasks]** - Personal folder - personal life admin, errands, home tasks
- **[General Tasks List]** - Thames Boilers folder - work tasks: quotes, invoices, customer enquiries

## Tags (Contexts)

### Location/Device
- `🏠 Home` - At home, physical tasks
- `Mac` or `Macbook Pro` - Computer work
- `☎️ Phone` - Calls to make  
- `📧 Email` - Emails to send
- `🏃‍♂️Errands` - Out and about

### Status
- `🪑(Waiting)` - Waiting for someone/something

### Energy/Type
- `💰 To Buy / Spare Money` - Purchases, needs budget

- `📺` - Video content

### Tools/Apps
- `ServiceM8` - Business management system

### Someday/Maybe Markers

- `Paraguay` or `Par` - Paraguay related

## Project Naming Conventions

- `[Bracketed Names]` - Single-action lists (ongoing containers)
- `[OnHold]` suffix - Paused projects
- Regular names - Active projects with defined outcomes

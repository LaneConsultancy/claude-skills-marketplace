## What's Included — In Plain English

**Annual boiler service** — A Gas Safe engineer visits once a year to test controls, inspect the heat exchanger, check for gas leaks or CO risks, and clean key components. Takes about an hour, keeps your boiler efficient, and keeps your manufacturer warranty valid.

**24/7 breakdown call-out** — When you call, you speak to someone who can actually help. We triage the problem, give you an honest timeframe, and dispatch an engineer. Every day of the year, bank holidays included.

**Parts and labour** — Basic covers up to £500/year in parts and labour, which handles the vast majority of common repairs. Premium has no cap — faulty pump, broken diverter valve, failed PCB — we cover the part and the engineer's time, no argument.

**No excess (Premium)** — Your monthly payment is your only payment. Nothing extra when you make a claim. Basic has a £60 excess per call-out.

**Central heating system cover (Premium)** — Covers the whole system: radiators, pipework, pump, thermostat. If a radiator valve seizes or a pump fails, you're still covered.

## Why smart thermostats actually save money (and why most installs don't deliver)

The average UK household spends around £1,300–£1,800 per year on heating. Studies from the Energy Saving Trust consistently show that a correctly configured smart thermostat reduces heating costs by 15–30%. On a £1,500 annual bill, that's £225–£450 back every year.

The word "correctly" does a lot of work in that sentence. A Nest or Tado installed without geofencing active, without proper away schedules, and without checking the TRV configuration will behave like an expensive version of your old dial thermostat.

We configure everything on the day — schedules, geofencing, hot water if applicable, and zone control if your system supports it.

## What you get from a professional installation

**A thermostat wired to your specific boiler** — Combi, system, heat-only — they all wire differently. We check compatibility before we arrive and bring the correct adapter plate if needed.

## Which smart thermostat is right for you?

### Nest Learning Thermostat (3rd Gen)
Best for homeowners who want the system to figure out their schedule without manual configuration. Learns your patterns in the first week. The strongest app of the three — Google Home native, clean, well-maintained. **Honest limitation:** no native multi-zone support.

### Hive Active Heating 2
Best for straightforward control, good app support, and an expandable ecosystem. Designed around British boiler wiring from the start — the receiver unit means wider system compatibility. The broadest UK smart home ecosystem: plugs, bulbs, sensors, cameras in one app. **Honest limitation:** schedule-based only, no learning/pattern detection.

### Tado Smart Thermostat V3+
Best for homes with multiple heating zones or plans to add per-room control. Multi-zone architecture is built in, not bolted on. Native Apple HomeKit support — no bridge required. Energy IQ shows monthly consumption vs. weather data. **Honest limitation:** full Auto-Assist geofencing requires a Care & Protect subscription (£2.99/month).

## Frequently asked questions

**Will this work with my combi boiler?** — All three are compatible with the vast majority of UK combis. Send us your boiler make and model before booking and we'll confirm compatibility the same day.

**Can I control upstairs/downstairs separately?** — Yes. Tado is cleanest for this: smart TRVs give independent targets per room. Nest uses Temperature Sensors (influences one thermostat, not true zones). Hive supports multi-zone via additional receivers and a wiring centre. If zone control is your goal, we'd recommend Tado.

**Does geofencing actually work, or does it drain battery?** — It works. Battery impact is minimal (2–5%/day) because it triggers on significant location changes, not constant GPS polling. The common failure is skipping location permissions during setup. We configure and test this before we leave.

**Which smart home platform does each one support?** — All three support Alexa and Google Assistant. Tado is the only one with native HomeKit. Nest integrates tightly with Google Home. Hive has the most developed UK ecosystem if you're adding plugs, sensors, and lighting. Tell us your platform and we'll recommend accordingly.

**My boiler is 15+ years old. Should I replace it first?** — If it's running reliably, a smart thermostat will reduce how often it fires and makes consumption easier to monitor — Tado's Energy IQ can flag unusual patterns that signal a developing fault. If internal components are degrading, the thermostat won't fix that. We'll give you an honest read during the pre-visit check.

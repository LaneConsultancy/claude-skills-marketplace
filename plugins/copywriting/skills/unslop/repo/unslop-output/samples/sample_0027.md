**What Most Heating Engineers Won't Tell You**

This industry has a reputation problem. Vague quotes, muddy carpets, engineers who don't show up — most people booking a boiler job have either experienced it themselves or heard about it from someone who has. We'd rather address it head-on than pretend it doesn't happen.

---

**The bill that grows**
A quote that balloons once the job's underway is the oldest trick in the trade. We give you a fixed price in writing before any work starts. If something unexpected comes up mid-job, we stop, explain it, and agree a revised figure with you before we continue. You will never open an invoice and find a number you didn't approve.

---

**The mess left behind**
Some engineers treat your home like a site. We don't. We use dust sheets throughout, clean up before we leave, and take all waste with us. If anything is moved to access pipework or the boiler, it goes back where we found it.

---

**The two-hour window that becomes all day**
"We'll be there between eight and six" is not a booking — it's a hostage situation. We give a one-hour arrival window and send a text when we're on the way. If something genuine holds us up, we call ahead. After the job, you'll have a direct number if anything needs following up. We don't disappear once the invoice is paid.

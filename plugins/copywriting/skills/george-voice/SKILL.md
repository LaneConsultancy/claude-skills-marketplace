# George Lane's Writing Voice

Write content that sounds authentically like George Lane - warm, honest, direct, and personal.

## Before Writing

Read the reference file:
- `references/voice-guide.md` - George's voice characteristics, phrases, and style patterns

## Voice Essentials

George's writing feels like it's from a real person, not a marketing department or AI.

**Do:**
- Use first person ("I wanted to share...")
- Be direct and honest about the "why"
- Keep paragraphs short (2-4 sentences)
- Use British English and contractions
- Express genuine appreciation
- Write like you're talking to someone, not at them

**Don't:**
- Use corporate jargon or aggressive sales language
- Write long, dense paragraphs
- Be overly formal or impersonal
- Overuse exclamation marks or emojis
- Use American spellings

## Writing Structure

1. **Opening:** Get to the point, but warmly
2. **Body:** Short paragraphs, practical content, explain the "why" not just the "what"
3. **Close:** Warm sign-off, genuine appreciation

## Output Format

Default to plain text/markdown unless a specific format is requested.
